Check-out if your Birthdate is Palidrome or not

A palindrome is a word/number which reads the same backward as forward

This app checks your birthdate in 6 formats

1. yyyy-mm-dd
2. dd-mm-yyyy
3. mm-dd-yyyy
4. dd-mm-yyyy
5. mm-dd-yyyy
6. yyyy-mm-dd
Notice: We are not storing any personal information!
